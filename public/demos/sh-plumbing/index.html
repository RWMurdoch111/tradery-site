About SH Plumbing & Heating

Efficient, reliable,
trustworthy work

SH Plumbing & Heating is a Manchester-based plumbing and heating company providing professional services to homeowners and landlords across the North West. Fully trained and qualified heating engineers, every job is handled to a high standard from first call to final sign-off.

Whether it's a new boiler, a leaky tap, a full bathroom fit, or a landlord safety check, we treat every customer's home with the respect it deserves. Competitive pricing, no surprises — just honest, technically competent work.

FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you need? Call us directly — always happy to chat.

07753 423 586
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes — free, no-obligation quotes for all jobs. For larger projects we'll visit in person; for smaller jobs we can often quote over the phone or from photos.
Are you Gas Safe registered?
Yes. SH Plumbing & Heating engineers are fully Gas Safe registered. You can verify our registration on the Gas Safe Register at any time.
Are you insured?
Yes — we hold £5 million public liability insurance, so your property is fully protected on every job.
How quickly can you start work?
Urgent plumbing and boiler issues are prioritised — often same day or next day. For planned bathroom refurbs and boiler installs, typical lead time is one to two weeks.
Do you do landlord gas certificates?
Yes — annual CP12 landlord gas safety certificates across all our coverage areas. Flexible scheduling around tenants and quick turnaround on paperwork.
What areas do you cover?
Based in Manchester, we cover most of the North West — including Salford, Didsbury, Chorlton, Stretford, Trafford, Stockport, Altrincham, Bury, and Bolton. We travel further afield for larger projects.
