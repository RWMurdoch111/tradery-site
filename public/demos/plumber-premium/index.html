About Mark Ellis Plumbing

Built on reliability,
finished with care

Mark Ellis Plumbing is a sole-trader plumbing and heating business based in Islington, North London. After 15 years working for larger contractors, Mark went independent in 2018 — bringing the same standard of work but with the personal service only a sole trader can offer.

Whether it's a dripping tap, a new boiler, or a full bathroom strip-out and rebuild, Mark handles the job himself from start to finish. No subcontractors, no surprises — just straightforward, quality plumbing at a fair price.

Leaks, bursts, pipe replacements, tap and toilet installations, and all routine plumbing repairs. Emergency call-outs available — fast response across Islington and surrounding areas.

Boilers & Heating

Gas Safe registered boiler installations, replacements, and servicing. Power flushing, radiator work, and full central heating systems. Worcester Bosch and Vaillant specialist.

Landlord Services

Annual gas safety certificates (CP12), boiler services, and reactive maintenance for rental properties. Flexible scheduling to work around tenants.

Wet Rooms

Design and installation of fully waterproofed wet rooms — tanking, tiling, linear drains, and thermostatic shower systems. Ideal for accessible bathrooms and modern refurbs.

Supply and installation of unvented hot water cylinders for improved flow rates and pressure. G3 qualified installation to Part L building regulations.

FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Call directly — always happy to chat.

Do you offer free quotes?
Yes — free, no-obligation quotes for all jobs. For larger projects Mark will visit in person; for smaller work he can often quote over the phone or via photos.
How quickly can you start work?
Emergency plumbing is prioritised — often same day or next day. For planned refurbishments and boiler installs, typical lead time is 1–2 weeks.
Are you Gas Safe registered?
Yes. Mark is Gas Safe registered (No. 123456) and fully insured with public liability cover. You can verify his registration on the Gas Safe Register website at any time.
Do you work on weekends?
Yes — Saturday appointments are available for planned work. Emergency call-outs are available seven days a week, including bank holidays.
Do you supply materials or should I?
Either works. Mark can supply all materials at trade prices, or work with sanitaryware and fittings you've chosen yourself. He'll advise on what suits your specification and budget.
What areas of London do you cover?
Based in Islington, Mark covers most of North and Central London — including Highbury, Canonbury, Finsbury Park, Holloway, Hackney, Stoke Newington, Camden, and Clerkenwell. He also travels further afield for larger projects.
